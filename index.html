Hearts of Iron IV Character Generator

This HOI4 character generator is a browser-based tool for Hearts of Iron IV modding. It generates country leaders, character definitions, ideologies, portrait references, localization entries, and recruit commands compatible with vanilla and modern HOI4 mods.

Designed for fast workflow and clean output, this tool helps modders create playable countries and leaders without manual scripting.
